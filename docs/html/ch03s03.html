Design Window

The design window shows the frame or panel you are creating in WYSIWYG mode and allow you to select a widget from the main window and to put it on a empty slot of a sizer. You can show the design window double-clicking in a frame's icon or dialog's icon in the tree window.

Figure 3.5. The Design Window

Clicking whit the right-button of the mouse on a widget you can access to the context-menu. Notice that the sizers, that are invisible elements, have a little gray "handle", that you can click to select the sizer or let the pop up menu appear.

The pop up menu is the same as you get in the Tree Window, as shown in Figure 3.3 or in Figure 3.4.
